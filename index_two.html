LOS ANGELES

Perch

Everyone's favorite rooftop restaurant, Perch! This place offers astounishing pastas and a variety of different French foods & brunch items that are out of this world.

Howlin' Ray's

Craving some authentic Nashville Hot Chicken? Look no further because you just landed at Howlin' Ray's! It is located right in the middle of Chinatown.

Daikokuya Little Tokyo

Hands down, the best Japanese Ramen I've ever had! It can be found on the outer rim of Little Tokyo.

ANAHEIM

Anaheim Packing District

Can't decide where to eat? Try out this food district that offers so many different kinds of foods & desserts. Great place to also socialize and hang out with your friend!

Honeymee

Have you ever heard of or tried honey comb icecream? This place has unique icecreams & milkshakes that come with a natural honey comb or infused honey! Try it out!

One Star Pizza & Chicken

Sure, fried chicken and pizza are great, but Korean fried chicken & pizza are just unexplainable. Try out this awesome combination and you will thank me later!

SAN DIEGO

Tacos El Gordo

Who dosn't love tacos!? Hands down, these will be some of the best tacos you try in your life!

Board & Brew

Craving a classic, delicious sandwich? Check this place out! Their sandwiches are delicious, especially with their delicious signature sauce!

SomiSomi

This desert location combines unique icecream flavors with a filled, doughly pastry! Yum!

IRVINE

Mokkoji Shabu Shabu

Do you enjoy hot pot? This place is by far the best and they offer a variety of high quality meats!

Urban Plates

Want something light & healthy, but delicious? This place offers different kinds of healthy & yummy foods!

Honey & Butter Macarons

This Macaron place is life changing! It can be found at the Irvine Spectrum and will change your perspective on desserts!
